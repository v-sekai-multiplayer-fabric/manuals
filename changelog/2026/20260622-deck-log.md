# V-Sekai Multiplayer Fabric Deck Log — 2026-06-22 (Monday)

Date: 2026-06-22

Split the `lean-predictive-bvh` proof monorepo into one repository per hexagon cluster, along the dependency seams the
lakefile already drew, and archived the monorepo. Updated the vertical-slice repository map to match.

## Added

- Six standalone Lean workspaces, one per hexagon cluster, each carrying its own `core/ports/adapters` tree, a lakefile
  that wires its siblings over `require ... from git`, and a CI gate that builds the production closure:
  [lean-shared-core](https://github.com/v-sekai-multiplayer-fabric/lean-shared-core), the dependency-free primitive
  types every other core requires;
  [lean-rebac-core](https://github.com/v-sekai-multiplayer-fabric/lean-rebac-core), the NoGod / ReBAC authorization
  core;
  [lean-spatial-oracle](https://github.com/v-sekai-multiplayer-fabric/lean-spatial-oracle), the predictive BVH and its
  R128 fixed-point with ghost expansion, SAH, and Hilbert broadphase, emitting `predictive_bvh.h` through the AmoLean
  codegen;
  [lean-humanoid-rom](https://github.com/v-sekai-multiplayer-fabric/lean-humanoid-rom), range-of-motion and IK
  constraints with the body-model and biomechanics citations;
  [lean-fabric-protocol](https://github.com/v-sekai-multiplayer-fabric/lean-fabric-protocol), the networking and SLA
  proofs (saturation, waypoint bounds, abyssal SLA); and
  [lean-interest-mgmt](https://github.com/v-sekai-multiplayer-fabric/lean-interest-mgmt), authority-interest and
  solve-order. The push order follows the dependency graph: shared-core and rebac-core first, then spatial-oracle, then
  fabric-protocol, then interest-mgmt, and each repository's lake build is green.

## Changed

- The [repository map](../../decisions/20260612-vertical-slice-repository-map.md) lists the predictive-oracle proofs as
  the six per-cluster repositories above, replacing the single `lean-predictive-bvh` entry under the wire, transport,
  and determinism specs.

## Removed

- The [lean-predictive-bvh](https://github.com/v-sekai-multiplayer-fabric/lean-predictive-bvh) monorepo is archived and
  read-only; its proofs now live in the six repositories above. The CITATIONS.bib moved whole to lean-humanoid-rom, the
  only cluster its body-model and biomechanics references cover.
